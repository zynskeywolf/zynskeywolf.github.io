The musicc

I mostly do trance and its derivatives, preferably with the "classic" and "euro-" prefixes, except when I don't. In that case, I mix everything from house and trance to synthwave and orchestral elements in the weirdest possible ways. Preferred tools are usually LMMS and ZynAddSubFx.

How acquire?

All of my solo releases (including Trancwolve and Steve Pixus aliases) are available free of charge, with lossless downloads where it's supported. Go to the Links page for all the links. The exact format can vary depending on the platform, check the profile/channel descriptions for details. If you need the original hi-res masters for whatever reason, just hit me up.

If you still feel an overpowering need to support me, you can do it through Bandcamp, highly appreciated :3

Licensing n stuff

You are free to include any of my tracks in videos and live broadcasts without my explicit permission, as long as you properly credit me. A simple "Artist - Title" line is fine, but linking the source is always appreciated.

The above applies to solo releases only and does NOT apply to remixes and collabs. In that case please ask me/us first.

Reuploading/redistributing parts or the entirety of any of the tracks by themselves (remixed or not) without my explicit permission is prohibited unless a CC license permitting it is indicated.
