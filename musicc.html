The musicc

I mostly do trance and its derivatives, preferably with the "classic" prefix. Even though I have made some exceptions, the Trancwolve alias is generally focusing on trance. Everything else still goes under Steve Pixus, where I do anything and everything, even different styles mixed and abused in the weirdest possible ways. The ProtoGenZ are an imaginary proot choir singing in some tracks, and Stimki Pawz is a made-up alias for remixing my own tracks. Don't question my logic here.

Weapon of choice is usually LMMS and ZynAddSubFx, occasionally a very few external plugins.

All of my solo releases (including all aliases) are free to download in lossless quality. You can find all the possible places at Links.

If you still feel an overpowering urge to support me, you can do it through Bandcamp, highly appreciated ^w^

Licensing

You are free to include any of my tracks in your own content (be it pre-recorded or live) without my explicit permission as long as proper credit is given. A simple "Artist - Title" line is enough, but linking the source is always appreciated.

The above applies to solo releases only and does NOT apply to remixes and collabs uploaded under my profile. In that case please ask me/us first.

Reuploading/redistributing parts or the entirety of the tracks by themselves (remixed or not) without my explicit permission is prohibited, unless a CC license permitting it is explicitly indicated at the official upload of the track.

Technical info for nerds

Formats available on different platforms:

  • Bandcamp: Red Book standard (44.1 kHz, 16-bit) unless otherwise noted
  • Audius: 320k MP3 before 2024, varied but lossless sice they started supporting it
  • Soundcloud (personal uploads): Varied but always lossless
  • YouTube (personal uploads): Source is the raw hi-res master directly muxed in
  • Spotify and everything else: Whatever they use, I feed them with Red Book

If you need the original hi-res masters for whatever reason, just hit me up.
